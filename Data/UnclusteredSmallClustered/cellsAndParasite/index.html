Arrow keys or ASWD keys to move left, right, back, and forward. Space bar to move up, B key to go down. K key to increase time, J key to decrease time. V key to increase movement speed, C to decrease. Z key to start autoplay, ZASWD to stop autoplay. M key to toggle mouse control on and off; mouse rotates camera. P key to toggle grid overlay. O key to toggle position and facing display of current camera. I key to toggle cell identification labels.
